Ronnie Barth is a multidisciplinary artist, painter, illustrator, and magician born and based in Southern California.

Ronnie's work intertwines abstraction, spiritualism, and surrealism, grasping the essence of what it means to experience the world rather than apprehending the subject itself. Her art seeks to embody the delicate interplay that rekindles a deep reverence for the natural world, inviting viewers to fall in love with its beauty, intricacy, and transcendence.

By gathering materials such as sand, dirt, and dust from the places she visits, she weaves the heart of the land into her work, creating an intimate dialogue between memory, material, and meaning.
